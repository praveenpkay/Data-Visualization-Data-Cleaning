Foreign Direct Investment In India - Sector & Financial Year-wise time-series data from 2000-2016.

As part of my self-paced #data learning,
I've built my first dashboard in #Tableau to answer the following KPIs.

1. Highest/Lowest value for FDI & its sector in the latest year.
2. Trend of overall FDI and its sector.
3. Grouping of sectors for comparison.
4. FDI in top 5 sectors.
5. Highest growth/decline of the sector in the last 5 years.
6. Secorts with respective variations.
7. Sector and its clusters.
8. Proportion of each sector in FDI.
9. Forecast for next 3 years.
